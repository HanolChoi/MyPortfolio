PROLOGUE

Once upon a time, there was a planet called Nevaeh.
It was always full of joy and happiness, and the positive energy surrounding the planet was powerful.
People used to call this energy as Lon,
which builds up day by day to enrich
the lives of planet Nevaeh. Lons gathered around at the center
of the planet where they formed into Bu, a small egg full of Nenaeh’s energy got bigger and bigger.

One fine afternoon, a dark storm completely
took over planet Nevaeh and for the first-time
people of Nevaeh felt the emotion of fear.

Nevaeh was always protected by
a safe shield from the Lons
however an unknown energy of darkness
flowed through and these energies
that gathered little by little
birthed an evil called Natas.

Natas began to absorb Lon from planet Nevaeh
and it began to express its evil energy of
anger, hate and jealousy.
Natas was the energy of fear and pain.
People of planet Nevaeh were directly affected by Natas;
they began to hate each other and tremble in fear.

The Masters of the village,
who were not yet affected by Natas,
considered Bu to be the last legacy of hope
to protect the planet Nevaeh.
Bu was sent to space with
the last efforts to preserve Nevaeh.
Bu will become the floating star of our universe
and slowly transform itself to a pure energy
called BUBBLY representing the good will of planet Nevaeh.

While wandering through space,
it discovers a planet in need of attention and care.
BUBBLY decides to save this planet
and takes the journey down to earth.
